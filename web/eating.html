Eating • Enhancing Nutrition and Wellness • Jul 5th '24

Dodging Dehydration

Even though we are not suffering through heat and humidity you should not think you are safe from dehydration. Dehydration can happen at any time, and it has serious consequences. Join us for a deep dive into dehydration with Stacy Kaminski, MS, RDN, CDN, CCDCES. Stacy Kaminski is a Registered Dietitian and Certified Diabetes Care and Education Specialist dedicated to providing comprehensive medical nutrition therapy at the NewYork-Presbyterian Weill Cornell Medicine Department of Surgery and the Center for Advanced Digestive Care. She can guide you through many ways you can protect your health and stay hydrated that do not entail drinking gallons of water. There will be plenty of time to answer your questions, so please plan to join us.
